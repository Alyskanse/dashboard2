Capstsone Draft

For my capstone project I am going to start making the portfolio that I am going to use to apply to the IMD program with. Since the application is due on April 1st, I want to start making my portfolio now.

In this portfolio I am going to include a home page that will be very simple. I have found inspiration from other portfolios I have looked at, and I like the idea of having my home page just be a single photo that covers the whole page. On top of the picture I want to have my name or some text.

I will also have a navbar in my portfolio that will link to different pages. For this navbar, I want to include buttons that change a different color when you hover over them. Right now I think I will make the buttons white when you are not hovering over it and light blue when you are hovering over it. Also, if you are on the home page for example, you will not be able to hover over the home button, it will just be a solid color (blue). All of the buttons will be on the right hand side, and on the left side will be my name or a logo that I make. I would also like to make the navbar semi-transparent but I have not figured out how to do this yet.

For the buttons on the navbar I know that I want to have a page titled "About Me" that will have info about me. I also plan to include on this page links to my github account and LinkedIn account. I will also include a page titled "Projects". This page will have all of my projects that I will use to apply to the IMD major. I will have different types of projects, so far I know that I want to include programming projects, art, and photography. On my projects page there will be more links/buttons to get to each type of projects. For example, if you want to view my photography there will be a button/link to get to another page that is just for photography. I also have an idea to create a separate website for my photography so I can show off more of my web design skills. If I decide to do this then I might have a separate button on my navbar just for photography.

I want the style of my portfolio to be very simple and minimalist so it does not look too busy. I plan to do this by only using a couple colors so the color pallet stays simple. Right now I am thinking of having one of the colors be light blue but that could change later. I also want my portfolio to look very clean. I am going to do this by making mh navbar very simple, and making my pages simple.
